About Me


My name is Vincent Pasquarelli, Vin for short, and I enjoy working in IT. I graduate college with a degree in Economics, and shortly after I took a job with United Healthcare working on IBM mainframe technology. I took a risk getting into IT, but what I ended up finding was my passion. I was a back-end developer for one of the largest claims processing platforms in the country. During my time there I found joy in programming but unfortunately mainframe tech is a dying art. To modernize my skillset, I am pivoting to Web Development and am currently enrolled in Rutger's full-stack course. Feel free to browse my portfolio to see some of the work I have completed.
